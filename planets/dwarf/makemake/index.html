Makemake

Discoverer:
Michael E. Brown
Discovered:
2005-03-31
Orbital Period:
309 years
Mean Radius:
739 km
Axial Tilt:
unknown

Makemake is a dwarf planet and perhaps the largest Kuiper belt object (KBO) in the classical population, with a diameter that is about 2/3 the size of Pluto.
